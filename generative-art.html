Telling stories

The serial comic is at the onset hardly compatible with generative methods. Also it is highly structured, and some central comic features are based on repetition. Echt Birt is an experiment aiming to take a pure code stance with the serial comic form, to see how far it carries.

Echt birt #1, AIV, 2016/10.
